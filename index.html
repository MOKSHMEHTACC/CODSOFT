Who we are

Welcome to SportsAdda Sports Club which has been made to provide
world-class facilities and infrastructure for the promotion of sports
and health.The SportsAdda Sports Club has been both breeding ground
and sporting haven for individual and team talent from across the
country.During the past few years,SportsAdda Sports Club has emerged
as one of the best facilities in the western suburbs of Mumbai in the
area of sports and health and is making strides to acquire a formidable
presence in the international arena also.The club has a swimming pool,
a fully equipped health club six acres of open ground for promoting
outdoor sports like cricket,football,volleyball,basketball,lawntennis etc.
